Privacy Policy

Inflow is an AI email assistant browser extension.

What we collect

  • Google account info you provide during sign-in (email address, and optionally profile picture).
  • Text you choose to submit to generate AI output (e.g., the prompt or email text you paste/use).
  • Subscription status (via Stripe) if you upgrade.

How we use data

  • To authenticate you and show your plan.
  • To generate AI outputs you request.
  • To operate billing and prevent abuse.

Third parties

We use third-party providers to operate Inflow, including Google (sign-in), Stripe (billing), Supabase (database), and OpenAI (AI processing).

Data retention

We do not aim to store your email contents. We may store account identifiers (like email) and plan status. Please avoid submitting highly sensitive personal data.

Security

We use reasonable safeguards, but no system is 100% secure.
